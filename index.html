Biography

I am a PhD candidate in Anthropology in the School of Human Evolution and Social Change at Arizona State University. I use computer simulations to answer questions at the intersection of anthropology and climate science. In particular, I study the feedbacks between climate change and cultural change over the past 10,000 years.
